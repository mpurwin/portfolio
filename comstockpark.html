The Work

60 years of
history told.

As a family business for over 60 years, there is a lot of history to be told. As cinematographer, editor, and occasional designer, it was crucial to show how Comstock Park Body Shop differentiates themselves from competitors.

60th Anniversary Logo

I had the honor of crafting a logo for Comstock Park Body Shop's 60th anniversary. The challenge was to encapsulate six decades of history and legacy into a compelling design. The final logo was placed on a banner at the front of their building and was featured on custom t-shirts worn by every employee.

Website Header Video

I produced a captivating header video for Comstock Park Body Shop's new website. This visual narrative serves as a quick, yet dynamic, introduction — welcoming visitors and online audiences to the business. From concept to execution, this video is a visual story of the unique identity and values of Comstock Park Body Shop.

Disinfectant Informational Video

I created an informative video shedding light on Comstock Park Body Shop's effective use of Transform Disinfectant to sanitize cars post-repair. This visual guide enhances safety protocols, providing valuable insights for both customers and the dedicated team.
